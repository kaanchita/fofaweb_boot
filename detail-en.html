Qualifications of creative work : Visual arts and Design

Type of creative work

Product design :
Jewelry design, Fashion design, Packaging design

Communication design :
Design character sets, Graphic design on packaging, Sign design, Pattern design, Corporate identity design, Character design, Print media design

Creative works in visual arts :
2D, 3D format, Visual arts works in contemporary media.

The Creative presentation format

1. Photos

2. Video Art

Presenter of creative work submits information

1. Concept Paper (form of IFA-2024)

2. Photos of creative work: size 16:9, resolution not less than 720p (Horizontal image)

3. Photos of the creator: size 4:3, resolution not less than 720p (Vertical image)

4. The creative work:

- Product design, Jewelry design, Fashion design, Packaging design, Communication design, Visual arts: 2D, 3D : Photos of Creative work: Quantity 3-5 images, resolution 720-1080p

- Visual arts works in contemporary media. : Video Art: size 16:9, file format MP4, resolution 720-1080p, duration 3-10 minutes

Remark :

- Except for material work such as raw material and synthetic material

- Moral and ethical declaration of the submitted work.

1. There is no academic plagiarism.

2. There is no moral and ethical harassing concerned.

3. There is no pirating of copyrights and illegal lawful.
