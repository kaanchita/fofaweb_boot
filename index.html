ABOUT US

The 11th International Festival of Arts (IFA) edition is a project that was held. Continuing from the 10th International Festival of Arts and culture to bring art, music and performance activities and promote knowledge and understanding of culture and fine arts to Thai society and the world. Disseminate the valuable arts and culture of the Thai people and the diversity of international cultures in the form of performances, music and art. Presentations are shared and inherited and foster shared identities as well as create new opportunities for creative development. Cross-cultural development and respect for cultural diversity encountered in the practices of living cultural heritage contributes to effective and sustainable strategies for restoring peace and stability in society.
